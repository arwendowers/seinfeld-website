Interesting Facts About Seinfeld

Seinfeld became one of the most iconic sitcoms of all time by turning ordinary daily life into comedy. Here are ten fun facts and memorable details about the legendary show.

Intersting Seinfeld Facts

1. It Was First Called The Seinfeld Chronicles

Before it became simply Seinfeld, the show started with a different title during its early development.

2. It Was First Called The Seinfeld Chronicles

Before it became simply Seinfeld, the show started with a different title.

3. It Became Known as a Show About Nothing

The show was famous for making comedy out of everyday conversations.

4. Jerry Played a Version of Himself

Jerry Seinfeld’s character reflected his real-life career as a stand-up comedian.

5. Larry David Co-Created the Series

Larry David worked with Jerry Seinfeld to create the show’s sharp humor.

6. The Puffy Shirt is in a Museum

The shirt from the Season 5 episode “The Puffy Shirt” is housed in the Smithsonian National Museum.

7. “Kramer” Wasn’t In The Pilot

In the pilot episode, Kramer’s name was actually “Kessler” to avoid legal issues with Kramer’s inspiration, Kenny Kramer.

8. Pop Culture Icons

Jerry, George, Elaine, and Kramer became some of the most recognizable sitcom characters ever.

9. Elaine’s New Yorker Cartoon Published

The cartoon Elaine submitted to The New Yorker in “The Cartoon” was actually published by the real-life magazine.
